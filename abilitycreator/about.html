Ability Creator

What is the Ability Creator?

The Ability Creator is free tool for ProjectKorra server owners that allows them to create their own custom abilities. The proccess is simple, straight forward and anyone can do it with ease!

What are the requirements?

There are a few things needed for the Ability Creator to work:

  • ProjectKorra 1.8.1 or above.
  • A web browser on your PC (will not work on mobile devices)
  • Java Development Kit *
* Is only needed to compile the ability into a jar. You can get any developer to do this for you.

Is there a tutorial on making moves with the Ability Creator?

Not yet but I plan on making one shortly! In the meantime, PM me on ProjectKorra if you get stuck somewhere!

What does "compiling" mean?

"Compiling the ability" means to turn the generated .java files into a single .jar file for you to use (the one you put in the abilities folder). To do this, you will need:

  • The Java Development Kit
  • Eclipse
  • Spigot 1.11
  • A copy of ProjectKorra 1.8.1 or above

Compiling a jar is pretty challenging for someone who has never done it before, so I suggest you take the generated files from the Ability Creator and give them to a developer to compile. I am also willing to compile it for you if you donate a few dollars to support this project! A few dollars goes a long way since I spend hours and hours of my time doing this for nothing in return. :)

$5 = 5 jars


Why do I have to compile it/why can't I just download a jar?

This is hosted on a free website, and the amount of things you can do with a free website is limited. I don't have a job and don't get any money from PK, so I can't afford to pay for premium services. Which is why I can do it for you if you're willing to donate a dollar or two as thanks :D

In technical terms, I would need PHP to compile a jar and github.io sites only offer HTML support. If I had access to PHP, it might have been possible to compile it server side but currently that isn't an option. In future, I may make another tool to do the work for you. That said, it takes a lot of work just to keep the ability creator up-to-date/working.

Where can I get started?

You must be on a computer to use the Ability Creator. Sorry!
